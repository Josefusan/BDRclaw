Questions, answered

Is this just another Instantly? +

No. Email is one of our seven channels. Email platforms give you send infrastructure — you still write templates, handle replies, and update the CRM. BDRclaw composes each message per prospect, quality-gates it with a second AI, handles the replies, and books the meeting — across email, LinkedIn, SMS, WhatsApp, Telegram, X, and Instagram.

Will it get my accounts banned? +

Account protection is built in: LinkedIn is capped at 20 invites per day, SMS respects a TCPA 2-touch maximum, X and Instagram run warm-replies-only (policy-safe), and every single message passes an AI quality gate before it sends. Unsubscribes are one click.

Do I need my own Twilio or LinkedIn account? +

Yes — for SMS and WhatsApp you connect your Twilio credentials, for Telegram your bot credentials, and LinkedIn uses your account with guided setup. We walk you through each connection during onboarding.

What CRMs do you support? +

GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Monday, and Salesforce. BDRclaw syncs prospects, replies, and meeting outcomes so your CRM stays the source of truth.

How does the free trial work? +

It's a 14-day free pilot with concierge onboarding — we set it up with you and campaigns are live within 48 hours. Email-first with capped sends, no credit card required. We onboard a limited number of pilots at a time. Start here.
